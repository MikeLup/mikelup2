Nielsen's 10 Usability Heuristics: Problems

When designing the app, some of our problems with the usability heuristics included visbility, help and documentation, and flexibility/ease of use

Nielsen's 10 Usability Heuiristics: Solutions

Our solutions to these problems included refining the color scheme to allow for maximum visibility and clarity, adding a selection for new users to follow to sign up, as well as clear menu choices relating to which part of the app you wish to use. We also added a recent searches and most searched options to expediate use of the app and learn from user behavior
